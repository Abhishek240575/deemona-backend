## 3. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

**YOU SHALL NOT use the Platform to:**

- Violate any local, state, national, or international law
- Engage in fraud, money laundering, or financial crimes
- Facilitate illegal transactions
- Infringe on intellectual property rights
- Violate privacy or data protection laws
- Engage in tax evasion or regulatory violations
- Support terrorist organizations or sanctioned entities

### 3.2 Unauthorized Access

**YOU SHALL NOT:**

- Access accounts or data belonging to others
- Attempt to bypass authentication or security measures
- Use stolen, shared, or unauthorized credentials
- Probe, scan, or test Platform vulnerabilities
- Access any part of the Platform you are not authorized to access
- Circumvent usage limits or restrictions

### 3.3 System Abuse

**YOU SHALL NOT:**

- Upload viruses, malware, ransomware, or harmful code
- Launch denial-of-service (DoS) or distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks
- Overload or interfere with Platform infrastructure
- Use automated scripts or bots (except where explicitly permitted)
- Scrape or extract data through unauthorized means
- Attempt to reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Platform

### 3.4 Data Misuse

**YOU SHALL NOT:**

- Upload data you do not have the legal right to use
- Upload personally identifiable information (PII) without proper consent
- Upload confidential information belonging to third parties
- Use the Platform to store or transmit stolen data
- Upload sensitive information (credit cards, SSNs, health records)
- Violate GDPR, CCPA, or other data protection regulations

### 3.5 Harmful Content

**YOU SHALL NOT upload or transmit:**

- Hate speech or content promoting discrimination
- Violent, graphic, or disturbing content
- Sexually explicit or pornographic material
- Content that harasses, threatens, or bullies others
- Defamatory, libelous, or slanderous statements
- Content that violates others' privacy rights

### 3.6 Commercial Misuse

**YOU SHALL NOT:**

- Resell or redistribute access to the Platform
- Use the Platform to compete with the Owner
- Share your account with multiple users (unless permitted by license)
- Use the Platform for purposes outside your subscription tier
- Violate license restrictions or usage limits

### 3.7 False Information

**YOU SHALL NOT:**

- Create accounts with fake, misleading, or impersonated identities
- Provide false information during registration
- Misrepresent your affiliation with any person or entity
- Upload intentionally misleading or fraudulent data
